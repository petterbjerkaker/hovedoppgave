Melhus Marathon 2024

General information and trail maps

The Melhus Marathon was established in 2024. The races are meant for anyone, and are not specific nor restricted to runners who are members of running clubs.
This initiative was brought forward as a result of the town Melhus’ rapid increase in population. Melhus Marathon stands as a symbol for uniting the town’s residents,
and to bring attention towards our fair town nationwide. Each of our races are up-to-date regarding security-, and medical attention-measures.
The use of headphones during the race is not permitted, due to the importance of being aware of your surroundings, and of messages conveyed during the races. Consumption of alcohol is not permitted on event grounds, along the trails, regarding both racers, and bystanders.
The Melhus Marathon is a family-friendly event.

Marathon

The marathon trail starts at Klett, which is located just south of Trondheim city.
The trail then proceeds in the south direction, towards the town of Melhus.
You continue through Melhus and pass the small towns of Søberg, Kvål, and Ler.
The last stage of the first half of the marathon trail goes from Ler to Lundamo.
When arriving at Lundamo, you turn and run the trail back, finishing where you
started at Klett. This trail is 42 kilometers.
The map to the right showcases the distances. From Klett to Lundamo you climb a total of approximately 116 height-meters.
The foundation on which you run is mostly asphalt.
The map is illustrated with three different symbols. The medic logo is where health personnel are stationed, the water drop shows water stations, and the music note is where live music will be played throughout the race.

Half-marathon

The half-marathon also starts at Klett, located just south of Trondheim city.
The trail then proceeds in the south direction, towards the town of Melhus.
You continue through Melhus and pass the small town of Søberg.
The last stage is from Søberg to Børsetsveen where you finish.
This trail is 21 kilometers, and the map to the right showcases the distance.
From Klett to Børsetsveen it is approximately 116 height-meters.
The foundation on which you run is mostly asphalt.
The map to the right is illustrated with three different symbols. The medic logo is where health personnel are stationed, the water drop shows water stations, and the music note is where live music will be played throughout the race.

The Melhus 10k

The Melhus Mile starts at the town center of Melhus, and ends at the small town of Leinstrand, just south of Klett.
For the most part in this trail you follow the main road, and the foundation is mostly asphalt.
The trail is mostly flat with no significant amount of height-meters, making it less challenging than the marathon trails.
This trail is 10 kilometers.
The map to the right is illustrated with three different symbols. The medic logo is where health personnel are stationed, the water drop shows water stations, and the music note is where live music will be played throughout the race.
